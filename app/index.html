Boids!

Boids = Birds

Theses little boxes are flying around mimicking behaviors of flocking birds. The rendering is happening in real time, that's why you can rotate the perspective, zoom in and out, and drag the box, all with your mouse. I am amazed at what Three.js could do.

I also deployed this little library dat.GUI so one can change the parameters used for rendering in real time. Pretty neat!

Look at them flock! If you stare at them long enough, the algorithm used for the flocking behaviour will eventually make the birds converge into one group.
